-ABOUT US-

Welcome to DiningSI, where we bring the vibrant flavors of authentic Chinese cuisine to Cebu City. Nestled in the heart of Cebu City, our restaurant is a culinary oasis, offering a delightful fusion of traditional recipes and modern interpretations

At DiningSI, we take pride in our rich culinary heritage and strive to deliver an unforgettable dining experience to our valued guests. With a commitment to quality ingredients, expert craftsmanship, and exceptional service, we invite you to embark on a gastronomic journey through the diverse regions of China, right here at our tables.

Our journey began with a passion for sharing the delectable flavors of our homeland. Inspired by family recipes passed down through generations, our chefs infuse each dish with love and authenticity, creating a symphony of flavors that tantalize the taste buds and warm the soul.

From our signature Peking Duck, roasted to perfection and served with all the traditional accompaniments, to our tantalizing array of dim sum delights, including delicate steamed dumplings and savory buns, every dish at DiningSI tells a story of culinary mastery and cultural heritage.

But our commitment to excellence goes beyond the kitchen. We believe that dining is an experience to be savored, and our warm and welcoming ambiance reflects our dedication to hospitality. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion with loved ones or simply craving a comforting meal, our team is here to ensure that every moment spent with us is nothing short of extraordinary.

As a family-owned and operated restaurant, we extend our heartfelt gratitude to our loyal patrons who have made DiningSI a cherished part of the community. Your continued support inspires us to continually innovate and elevate our offerings, ensuring that every visit is an adventure for the senses.

Join us at DiningSI and discover the true essence of Chinese cuisine. From the first bite to the last, let us transport you to a world of culinary delights and cultural wonders. We look forward to welcoming you with open arms and serving you an unforgettable dining experience.
